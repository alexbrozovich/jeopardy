1. George Washington
    Years: 1789 - 1797 (2 terms)
    Previous job: Commander-in-Chief of the continental army
    Party: None
    Wife: Martha Dandridge
    Vice President: John Adams
    Notable events:
        - Farewell address of 1796: advocated "free security" and warned against involvement in international politics
        
2. John Adams
    Years: 1797 - 1801 (1 term)
    Previous job: Vice President
    Party: Federalist
    Vice President: Thomas Jefferson
    Wife: Abigail Smith
    Notable events:
        - XYZ Affair: Envoy was sent to France to ask for compensation for attacks on American shipping. 
                      Envoy was kept waiting and France refused to conduct diplomatic business without taking huge bribes
        - Alien and Sedition Acts: Made it harder for an immigrant to become a citizen
                                   Allowed the president to imprison and deport "dangerous" non-citizens
                                   Criminalized false statements critical of the federal government
                                   Laid groundwork for WWII internment camps
                                   
3. Thomas Jefferson
    Years: 1801 - 1809 (2 terms)
    Previous job: Vice President
    Party: Democratic Republican
    Vice President: Aaron Burr / George Clinton
    Wife: Martha Wayles
    Notable events: 
        - Louisiana Purchase (1803): Purchased French land from Napoleon, doubling the size of the US
        - Lewis and Clark expedition (1804-1806)
        - Trail of Tears: affected mostly Cherokee / Shawnee
    
4. James Madison
    Years: 1809 - 1817 (2 terms)
    Previous job: Secretary of Stae
    Party: Democratic Republican
    Vice President: George Clinton (died in office) / Elbridge Gerry (died in office)
    Wife: Dolley Payne Todd
    Notable events:
